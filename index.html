BobBot SMS Assistant Opt-In

BobBot is Robert Hicks’s conversational SMS assistant for requested replies, task updates, reminders, and MMS/image responses.

How to opt in

Robert Hicks provides this number directly to people who request SMS access. To consent, send the keyword:

START

to:

(833) 365-9105

By texting START, you agree to receive BobBot SMS Assistant conversational replies, requested task updates, reminders, and MMS responses from Robert Hicks. Message frequency varies based on your requests. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out.

Consent rules

  • No purchased, rented, scraped, or third-party contact lists are used.
  • Messages are sent only after the user texts START or directly requests SMS access.
  • Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
